What are you wearing?

Welcome to PromApp

If your school's prom isn't on PromApp yet, you can create one yourself! When you have picked a dress you are going to buy, you can register that dress for a prom. Once a dress has been registered, PromApp will make sure no one else registers the same dress for the same prom.

Profile

Your profile lets you keep track of dresses that you like, and also follow a prom page for your school.

Registration

When you register your dress for a prom, no one else on PromApp will be allowed to register it for the same one.
